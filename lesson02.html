Hick's Law

Floor Plans
FloorPlans.com follows Hick's Law by featuring a filter feature on its homepage. Users can narrow down their choices to better navigate the website.

Visual Hierarchy

Blue Bell Ice Cream
Blue Bell Ice Cream's website takes advantage of the principle of visual hierarchy to make their page appealing. The viewer is drawn to the huge picture first, then the heading, then the logo.

Fitt's Law

Noverantale
This shop follows Fitt's Law by making the buttons at the bottom large and easy to find. (It also makes good use of contrast to make the buttons and the header to stand out from the main body of the page.)
