Target Audience

This is a weather site seeking to cater to the southeastern area of the state of Idaho. Users who will be accessing the site are those who are residents in the surrounding region, or visitors planning a trip to the general area. Users are of varying ages who have easy Internet access.

Personas

Robert is a freshman at Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg. He is originally from the East Coast. On his break, his classmates invite him to visit Bear Lake. Being new to the area, he wonders how he would prepare for the trip. Is it going to be cold? Should they camp overnight?

Ashley is from Las Vegas, Nevada. She and her friends are fans of the movie Napoleon Dynamite. They wish to visit Preston, Idaho, where the movie was filmed. Ashley, who isn't used to the snow, decides to check the weather conditions for the weekend. She also wants to read about Preston itself.

Scenarios

  • Should I drive this weekend from Rexburg to the Salt Lake area for Christmas?
  • Which weekend should we plan a stake youth activity to Bear Lake?
  • Should I wear my coat today?
  • Can I take my children to a park playdate on Tuesday afternoon?
  • Should I attach the snow chains on my car for tomorrow?
